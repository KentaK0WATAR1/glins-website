About GLINS

Mission

The Global Logistics Intermordal Network Simulation (GLINS) project delivers high-resolution forecasts of global cargo flows and quantifies the impact of policies on international shipping networks.

Research Domains

  • Trade-flow forecasting and scenario analysis
  • Environmental regulation & decarbonisation impacts
  • Optimisation of liner-service networks
